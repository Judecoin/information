# Judecoin Decentralized Wallet V1.3.2 Android Version Is Online

> **Date:** July 6, 2023  
> **Category:** Notice

The Flutter-compatible Cardano decentralized wallet version is online.

The next wallet version will add the use of biometrics to unlock stack wallets.

The Judecoin team uses Flutter to develop Cardano's official undeveloped decentralized Cardano wallet, and will submit it to Cardano official and Cardano open source code.
