# Why buy $JUDE?

JUDE is the native currency of the Judecoin network. It is used for private transactions, value transfer, network participation, service node operation, atomic swaps, and ecosystem development.

JUDE is the core unit of value inside Judecoin.

## Private digital cash

JUDE is private digital cash.

Judecoin is designed to protect transaction information by default, helping users send and receive value without exposing their full financial activity on a transparent public ledger.

With JUDE, users can transact privately, directly, and globally.

## Censorship resistance

JUDE is built for censorship-resistant value transfer.

Because Judecoin is decentralized, users can transact without relying on banks, payment platforms, or centralized intermediaries.

Judecoin gives users a way to move value even when traditional financial systems restrict access, freeze accounts, or limit transfers.

## Become your own bank

JUDE gives users sovereign control over their money.

Users can hold, send, and receive JUDE through their own wallets. They do not need permission from a bank, payment company, or centralized institution to access their funds.

This is one of the core purposes of decentralized digital currency.

## Fixed supply

JUDE is a scarce digital asset with a known fixed supply.

The Judecoin protocol ensures that no more than 87 million coins will exist.

Traditional currencies can be expanded by central authorities. JUDE has a fixed supply defined by protocol.

## Fungibility

Fungibility means each unit of a currency should be treated equally.

Judecoin’s privacy-oriented design helps prevent visible transaction history from being used to reject, censor, or label coins.

This supports JUDE as electronic cash.

## Low-cost global payments

JUDE can be used to send value anywhere in the world.

Judecoin is designed for fast, low-cost, direct digital payments. The network operates continuously, allowing users to transact 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

No transaction is too big or too small.

## Service node participation

JUDE is connected to network participation.

With Proof of Stake and service nodes, eligible participants can use JUDE to support the operation and security of the Judecoin network.

Service node operators help maintain decentralized infrastructure, support network stability, and participate in long-term ecosystem development.

Judecoin adopts atomic swap technology to support cross-chain transactions and communication between blockchain networks.

As atomic swap tools develop, JUDE can interact with other blockchain ecosystems without relying entirely on centralized intermediaries.

This gives JUDE an important role in cross-chain value transfer.
