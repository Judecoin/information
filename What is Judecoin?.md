# What is Judecoin?

Judecoin is a privacy-focused blockchain network built for private digital payments, decentralized participation, and long-term ecosystem infrastructure.

At its core, Judecoin is designed to protect user privacy while enabling secure value transfer on a verifiable blockchain network. It gives users a way to send, receive, store, and participate in digital value without relying on fully transparent transaction systems that expose financial activity to public tracing.

JUDE is the native currency of the Judecoin network. It is used for transactions, staking, service node participation, reward distribution, and future ecosystem activity.

Judecoin began with the foundation of private digital currency, but its direction is broader than payment alone. It is evolving into a network supported by Proof of Stake, service nodes, wallet infrastructure, atomic swap development, cross-chain value movement, and future ecosystem applications.

## Privacy as the Foundation

Privacy is one of the core foundations of Judecoin.

Many blockchain networks make transaction data publicly visible by default. While this transparency can support public verification, it can also expose user activity, transaction patterns, and address relationships.

Judecoin is designed to provide stronger transaction privacy while maintaining a verifiable blockchain network. This makes it suitable for users who value financial confidentiality, censorship resistance, and decentralized peer-to-peer value transfer.

For Judecoin, privacy is not only a feature. It is part of the network’s identity and long-term direction.

## JUDE as the Native Network Asset

JUDE is the native currency of the Judecoin network.

It is used to transfer value, participate in staking, operate or contribute to service nodes, receive network rewards, and support future ecosystem activity.

As Judecoin develops, JUDE is not only a transferable digital asset. It also becomes part of the network’s participation model. Through staking and service node participation, JUDE holders can take part in supporting the infrastructure of the network and receive rewards according to the network rules.

This gives JUDE a role beyond simple payment. It connects users, network participation, service node infrastructure, and ecosystem development.

## Proof of Stake and Service Nodes

Judecoin is supported by Proof of Stake and Judecoin Service Nodes.

Service Nodes provide underlying support for the Judecoin network. They help strengthen network availability, scalability, and decentralized infrastructure while allowing operators and participants to receive rewards according to the network rules.

To operate a full Judecoin Service Node, the required staking amount is 23,600 JUDE. After the staking transaction and registration process are accepted by the network, the service node becomes active and begins contributing to network operations.

Through service node staking, JUDE holders can participate directly in the network instead of only holding or transferring the currency. This creates a stronger relationship between the network and its long-term participants.

Judecoin’s Proof of Stake and service node model helps the network move toward a more participatory, reward-based, and infrastructure-driven system.

## Shared Participation

Judecoin also supports broader participation through shared service node staking.

Shared service nodes allow multiple participants to combine smaller stake contributions toward the full service node staking requirement. Rewards are distributed according to each participant’s stake contribution and the applicable network rules.

This makes participation more accessible to users who want to support the network and receive rewards without operating a full server by themselves.

Shared staking helps expand participation, strengthen community involvement, and support the decentralization of the Judecoin network.

## Wallets as the User Entry Point

Wallet infrastructure is an important part of Judecoin.

Judecoin wallets allow users to store, send, receive, and manage JUDE. As the network develops, wallets also become an entry point for staking, service node participation, multi-chain access, and future ecosystem functions.

A strong wallet layer makes Judecoin easier to use for everyday users, service node operators, developers, and ecosystem participants.

The wallet direction is not only about holding and transferring JUDE. It is also about making the network more accessible, usable, and connected to future applications.

## Atomic Swaps and Cross-Chain Value Movement

Judecoin is moving toward a broader cross-chain direction.

Atomic swap development is an important part of this direction. Atomic swaps allow value to move between different blockchain networks without relying on traditional centralized intermediaries.

This helps Judecoin connect with broader blockchain liquidity and settlement infrastructure while preserving its focus on privacy and decentralized value transfer.

As blockchain ecosystems become increasingly multi-chain, Judecoin’s role can expand beyond a single-chain payment network. It can become a privacy-focused network that connects users, wallets, service nodes, and cross-chain value movement.

## Research, Engineering, and Community

Judecoin is supported by a global community of researchers, core developers, engineering contributors, service node operators, and ecosystem participants.

Over time, more than 500 developers have contributed to the Judecoin project, including 30 core developers. Together, the research lab, core development team, and community developers continue to advance Judecoin’s work in privacy, network security, Proof of Stake infrastructure, wallet access, atomic swap development, cross-chain capability, and long-term ecosystem growth.

This combination of research, engineering, and community participation helps Judecoin evolve from a privacy-focused digital currency into a broader blockchain infrastructure network.

## Building a Broader Ecosystem

Judecoin is not limited to one wallet, one payment function, or one single use case.

Its long-term direction is to build a privacy-centered blockchain infrastructure where private payments, JUDE staking, service node operations, wallet access, atomic swaps, scalability improvements, developer resources, and ecosystem applications work together as one network.

This means Judecoin is developing from a privacy digital currency into a broader infrastructure layer for participation, value transfer, and ecosystem growth.

The goal is to make Judecoin easier to use, easier to build on, and easier to participate in across different groups, including users, holders, service node operators, developers, technical teams, and community members.

## Why Judecoin Matters

Judecoin matters because it brings privacy, participation, research, engineering, and infrastructure into one network.

It gives users a private way to transfer value.  
It gives JUDE holders a way to participate in network operations through staking.  
It gives service node operators a role in supporting decentralized infrastructure.  
It gives developers and researchers a foundation for privacy, security, wallets, atomic swaps, cross-chain interaction, and future applications.

In simple terms, Judecoin is not only a currency for private transactions.

It is an evolving blockchain network built around privacy, JUDE participation, service node infrastructure, wallet access, research-driven engineering, cross-chain capability, and long-term ecosystem growth.
