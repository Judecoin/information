# Judecoin Roadmap

The goal of Judecoin is to become sound money that is usable by everyone in the world. This is a civilization-changing technology that will dramatically increase human freedom and prosperity.

This roadmap is intended to provide high-level technical direction and enable different technical teams to work together toward a common goal for advancing Judecoin.

The role of developers in furthering this goal is to produce high-quality professional software that serves the needs of users, miners, and merchants. We strive for continuous technical improvement, producing reliable products that provide a solid foundation for Judecoin.

The basic design of Judecoin is sound. However, this does not mean it is perfect. It is prudent to make incremental improvements to the system through technically sound design and careful engineering.

By implementing optimizations and protocol upgrades, atomic interchange technology will allow for complete private online and offline transactions that extend many orders of magnitude beyond current limits.
