# Core Technologies of Judecoin

> **Date:** January 11, 2021  
> **Category:** Education

Atomic swap is a technology that enables trustless peer-to-peer transactions between different types of digital assets. It belongs to cross-chain technology.

The reason it is called an atomic swap is that the exchange action is either completed entirely or not completed at all. The exchange process has atomicity.

This transaction process, also known as atomic cross-chain transactions, is based on smart contracts that allow users to exchange desired tokens directly from their crypto wallets.

Thus, atomic exchange is essentially a peer-to-peer transaction across chains.

In order to meet the needs of the development of the Internet of Things, Judecoin adopts advanced atomic interchange technology based on CryptoNote's underlying protocol.
