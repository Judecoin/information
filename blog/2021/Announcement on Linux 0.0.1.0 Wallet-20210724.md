# Announcement on Linux 0.0.1.0 Wallet

> **Date:** July 24, 2021  
> **Category:** Release

With the development efforts of the Judecoin community technical team, the Linux 0.0.1.0 wallet has been officially launched.

The Judecoin technical team will continue to develop further based on the Linux wallet, and plans to launch a Windows version wallet in the future.

Wallet functions and interfaces will continue to be updated and developed to meet the participation and needs of community users.
