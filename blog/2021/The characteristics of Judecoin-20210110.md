# The Characteristics of Judecoin

> **Date:** January 10, 2021  
> **Category:** News

Judecoin features:

1. Judecoin is a leading cryptocurrency developed based on the underlying technology of blockchain. The decentralized distributed ledger and blockchain foundation help support scalability.

2. Judecoin is based on the CryptoNote protocol. Significant algorithmic differences help obscure blockchain transactions, with a focus on private and censorship-resistant transactions. Transactions are confidential and untraceable, helping protect user information in each transaction.

3. Judecoin is an open-source community. It differs from Bitcoin and derivative cryptocurrencies through the RandomJDX differential algorithm, and aims to attract leading cryptocurrency researchers and engineering talent from around the world.

4. Judecoin adopts the latest blockchain atomic exchange technology to realize cross-chain transactions with other blockchains, and to achieve broader communication between blockchain networks, forming the interconnection of everything.
