# Announcement on the Launch of Judecoin Decentralized Mobile Wallet

> **Date:** March 2, 2022  
> **Category:** Notice

In order for community users to manage Judecoin more safely, conveniently, and efficiently, the decentralized mobile wallet will be officially launched on March 5, 2022.

Community users are reminded to properly keep their wallet seeds to prevent loss.
