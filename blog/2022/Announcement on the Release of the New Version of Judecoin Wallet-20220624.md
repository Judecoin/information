# Announcement on the Release of the New Version of Judecoin Wallet

> **Date:** June 24, 2022  
> **Category:** Notice

The Judecoin community will optimize and upgrade some functions of the existing wallet version in the near future.

The community will also test the wallet to complete BTC-related functions in July.

After the relevant function testing is completed, the new wallet version will include BTC-related wallet functions and applications.

After the release of the new wallet version, Judecoin community users are encouraged to actively download and experience the new version.

Judecoin Team
