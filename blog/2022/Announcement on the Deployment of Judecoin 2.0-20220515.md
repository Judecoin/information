# Announcement on the Deployment of Judecoin 2.0

> **Date:** May 15, 2022  
> **Category:** Notice

The Judecoin community is changing its consensus mechanism from Proof of Work (PoW) to Proof of Stake (PoS) to enable the transition from Judecoin 1.0 to Judecoin 2.0.

This has always been part of the community plan, because it is a key part of the community strategy to realize the expansion of Judecoin and an important part of ecological application through upgrading.

Its characteristics are:

1. **Improved energy efficiency**  
   Users do not need to consume a large amount of energy to mine blocks.

2. **Lower barriers and lower hardware requirements**  
   Users do not need excellent hardware to gain the opportunity to build new blocks.

3. **Stronger decentralization**  
   Proof of Stake (PoS) can provide more nodes in the network.

4. **Stronger support for shard chains and cross-chain realization**  
   This is a key upgrade for network expansion and future related functions.

5. **Future ecosystem expansion**  
   In the future, smart contracts, decentralized finance (DeFi), decentralized autonomous organizations (DAO), non-fungible tokens (NFT), swaps, and other applications will be implemented on the basis of Judecoin 2.0.
