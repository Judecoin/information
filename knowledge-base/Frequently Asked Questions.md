# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Judecoin?

Judecoin is a leading cryptocurrency focused on private and censorship-resistant transactions. The public verification nature of most cryptocurrencies allows anyone in the world to track transaction activity. Judecoin adopts atomic swap technology to support cross-chain transactions while ensuring data security and privacy.

## How is Judecoin different from other digital currencies?

1. Judecoin is developed based on blockchain technology. Its decentralized distributed ledger provides a foundation for secure and scalable digital transactions.

2. Judecoin is based on the CryptoNote protocol. Its privacy-oriented design helps obscure blockchain transactions and supports confidential, untraceable, and censorship-resistant transactions.

3. Judecoin is an open-source community project. It uses the RandomJDX algorithm and aims to attract cryptocurrency researchers, developers, and engineering contributors from around the world.

4. Judecoin adopts atomic swap technology to support cross-chain transactions with other blockchains and broader communication between blockchain networks.
