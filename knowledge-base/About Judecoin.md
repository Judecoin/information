# About Judecoin

## A Brief Introduction

Judecoin is a leading cryptocurrency focused on private and censored matters. The public verification nature of most cryptocurrencies allows anyone in the world to track your money. In addition, the connection between your financial records and your personal identity can compromise your security. To avoid this, Judecoin uses powerful cryptography to create a network that allows all parties to interact without revealing the sender, receiver, or transaction amount. Like other cryptocurrencies, Judecoin has a distributed ledger that all participants can download and verify themselves. However, Judecoin uses a series of mathematical techniques to hide all privacy details and prevent any blockchain tracking. No one can see anyone else's account balance, and transactions cannot disclose the source of the transfers. Users can enjoy the transparency and security of blockchain without bearing all the risks of the financial system. Judecoin adopts the unique atomic exchange technology to realize online and offline cross-chain transactions on the basis of ensuring data security and privacy.

## Technical Information

Here are some basic technical information about Judecoin. For more details, please download the relevant information or contact the community.

### Proof of Work

Judecoin uses RandomJDX (an ASIC created by members of the Monero community to resist CPU friendly POW algorithm) to make the use of mining specific hardware infeasible. The Judecoin abandoned the variant of CryptoNight and its algorithm used in Monero coin before. There is more information in GitHub.

### Blocks

Create a new block every about 3 minutes. Instead of a maximum block size, there are block reward penalties and dynamic block sizes to ensure dynamic scalability.

### Key Privacy Enhancement Technologies

- Circular elliptic curve
- Stealth address
- Ring signature
- Trading via Tor / I2P
