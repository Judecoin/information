# What is JUDE?

JUDE is the native digital currency of the Judecoin network.

Judecoin is a leading cryptocurrency focused on private and censorship-resistant transactions.

Most existing cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin and Ethereum, have transparent blockchains. This means anyone in the world can publicly verify, analyze, and trace transactions. In addition, the sending and receiving addresses of these transactions may be linked to a person's real-world identity.

Judecoin uses powerful cryptography to protect sending addresses, receiving addresses, and transaction amounts.

## Confidential and Untraceable Transactions

Judecoin transactions are confidential and cannot be traced.

By default, each Judecoin transaction hides the sending address, receiving address, and transaction amount. This always-on privacy means that the activity of each Judecoin user enhances the privacy of all other users.

JUDE is fungible. Because of obfuscation, JUDE is not tainted by its involvement in previous transactions. This means JUDE can always be accepted without the risk of censorship.

## Powerful Cryptography

Judecoin uses powerful cryptography to create a network that allows all parties to interact without revealing the sender, receiver, or transaction amount.

Like other cryptocurrencies, Judecoin has a distributed ledger that all participants can download and verify themselves. However, Judecoin uses a series of mathematical techniques to hide privacy details and prevent blockchain tracking.

No one can see anyone else's account balance, and transactions do not disclose the source of transfers.

## Privacy and Security

Judecoin adopts the underlying protocol of CryptoNote V2.0, which protects the privacy of the sender and receiver in transactions and achieves complete privacy.

Key privacy enhancement technologies include:

- elliptic curve cryptography;
- private address technology;
- ring signature technology;
- transactions via Tor / I2P.

Private address technology hides the actual transaction address and provides privacy protection for the receiver.

Ring signature technology provides privacy protection for the sender. It uses untraceable co-signatures to obfuscate the signature of digital token transactions by mixing the actual sender with decoy signatures from the blockchain network.

## Atomic Swaps

Judecoin adopts advanced blockchain atomic swap technology to realize online and offline cross-chain transactions while ensuring data security and privacy.

Atomic swaps allow users to exchange digital assets across different blockchains without relying on a trusted third party, such as a cryptocurrency exchange company, and without needing to trust the person on the other side of the swap.

Judecoin atomic swap development includes cross-chain exchange between Judecoin and Bitcoin, Judecoin and Ethereum, and broader atomic exchange between different blockchain systems.

Atomic swap technology opens value transfer channels between chains, organically connects different blockchains, and makes the whole blockchain ecosystem more complete.

## Electronic Cash

Judecoin is electronic cash that can be used to pay quickly and cheaply around the world.

With Judecoin, users can send money to anyone anywhere in the world, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Like the Internet itself, the network is always on. No transaction is too big or too small, and users do not need anyone's commitment or approval.

There is no multi-day retention period and no risk of fraudulent refunds. Judecoin is safe from capital controls, which are measures that sometimes restrict the circulation of traditional currencies in countries experiencing economic instability.

## Fixed Supply

The Judecoin protocol ensures that no more than 87 million JUDE will exist.

JUDE has a known fixed supply and represents a scarce digital currency.

## Open and Permissionless Network

Judecoin is an open and permissionless network.

It allows people to interact without central interference. It is decentralized, voluntary, and non-invasive.

Judecoin is designed to support private and censorship-resistant transactions.

## Open Community

Judecoin is a grassroots community that attracts the world's best cryptocurrency researchers and engineering talent.

More than 500 developers have contributed to the Judecoin project, including 30 core developers. The forums and chat channels are very active.

Judecoin's research lab, core development team, and community developers have been pushing the frontiers of cryptocurrency privacy and security.
